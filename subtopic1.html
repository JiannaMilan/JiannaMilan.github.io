GIS Resources
https://www.nypl.org/collections/nypl-recommendations/guides/gis A directory from the New York Public library of GIS Online resources. It contains tools to learn about GIS, free GIS software, and data and resources from reputable GIS firms such as the federal government and Cornell's GIS department.
https://libguides.mit.edu/gis/unitedstates An online directory from the Massachusetts Institute Of Technology that contains links to US GIS data nationwide. It focuses on satellite imagery, geospacial data, and national maps.
